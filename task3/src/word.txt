Real Madrid secured three important points before the international break by beating Osasuna at the Santiago Bernabéu despite injuries to Rodrygo, Militão and Lucas Vázquez in the first half. Vini Jr. was instrumental in the victory, grabbing another hat-trick, and Bellingham was also on target for the Whites. Asencio made his official debut for Madrid and provided the assist for the English midfielder to make it 2-0.
